Frailty Dataset:

This dataset has,
	Columns - 5
	Records - 10
	
Observations,
	Height is provided in Inches
	Weight is provided in Pounds
	Age is provided in Years
	Grip Strength is provided in Strength in Kilograms
	Fraility is provided in  polar structure (Yes/NO)
		Yes -> Presence of frailty symptons
		No -> Absence of frailty symptons
