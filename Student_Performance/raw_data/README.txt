Student Academic Performance dataset,
	Gender - denotes the biological sex of students, categorized as either female or male.
    Ethnicity - denotes the racial or ethnic backgrounds of students, classified into groups labeled A, B, C, or D.
    Parental Education Level - signifies the educational attainment of students' parents or guardians.
    Meal Plan - indicates the type of meal provision for students, categorized as standard, free, or reduced.
    Test Preparation Participation - indicates whether students have undertaken preparatory courses for examinations.
    Mathematics Score - denotes the numerical assessment of students' performance in mathematics.
    Reading Score - denotes the numerical assessment of students' performance in reading comprehension.
    Writing Score - denotes the numerical assessment of students' performance in written expression.
